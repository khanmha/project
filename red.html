Red Velvet Cake Recipe

IngredientS

Cake!
  1. 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour.
  2. 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar.
  3. 1 teaspoon baking soda.
  4. 2 large eggs, room temperature.
  5. 1 teaspoon salt.
  6. 1 teaspoon cocoa powder.
  7. 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil.
  8. 1 cup buttermilk, room temperature.
  9. 2 tablespoons red food coloring.
  10. 1 cup hot water.
  11. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
  12. 1 teaspoon white vinegar.
Frosting!
  1. 1 pound (2 cups) cream cheese, softened.
  2. 1/2 pound (2 sticks) butter, softened.
  3. 4 cups powdered sugar, sifted.
  4. 2 teaspoons vanilla extract.

InstructionS

Preheat and Prepare!
  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
Dry Ingredients!
  1. In a medium bowl, sift together flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder.
Wet Ingredients!
  1. In a large bowl, gently beat together the vegetable oil, buttermilk, eggs, food coloring, vanilla, and vinegar using an electric mixer.
Combine!
  1. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, and mix until just combined.
  2. Gradually add the hot water, mixing until smooth.
Bake!
  1. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans.
  2. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  3. Cool the cakes in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove them from the pans to cool completely on a wire rack.
Frosting
  1. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and butter until smooth.
  2. Gradually add the powdered sugar, beating until light and fluffy.
  3. Mix in the vanilla extract.
Assemble
  1. Place one cake layer on a serving plate.
  2. Spread a generous layer of frosting on top.
  3. Place the second cake layer on top and frost the top and sides of the cake.
Serve
  1. Decorate as desired and serve!
