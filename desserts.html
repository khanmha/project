Chee$eCake Recipe

IngredientS

For the Base!
  1. 200g digestive biscuits
  2. 100g unsalted butter, melted
For the Cheesecake Filling!
  1. 600g cream cheese, at room temperature
  2. 250g caster sugar
  3. 200ml sour cream
  4. 3 large eggs
  5. 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. 1 tbsp plain flour

InstructionS

Prepare the Base!
  1. Crush the digestive biscuits into fine crumbs.
  2. Mix the melted butter with the biscuit crumbs until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture firmly into the base of a 23cm (9 inch) springform tin.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
Preheat the Oven!
  1. Preheat your oven to 160°C (320°F).
Make the Cheesecake Filling
  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth.
  2. Add the caster sugar and continue to beat until combined.
  3. Add the sour cream, eggs, vanilla extract, and plain flour. Mix until smooth.
Bake the Cheesecake!
  1. Pour the cheesecake filling over the chilled biscuit base.
  2. Smooth the top with a spatula.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for about 50-60 minutes, or until the cheesecake is set but still has a slight wobble in the center.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for about 50-60 minutes, or until the cheesecake is set but still has a slight wobble in the center.
Chill the Cheesecake!
  1. Once the cheesecake has cooled to room temperature, chill it in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight.
Serve
  1. Remove the cheesecake from the tin.
  2. Top with fresh berries or fruit compote, if desired.
  3. Slice and enjoy!
