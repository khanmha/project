BakeWell Tart Recipe

IngredientS

For the pastry!
  1. 200g plain flour
  2. 2 tbsp icing sugar
  3. 100g cold butter, cubed
  4. 1 egg yolk
  5. 2-3 tbsp cold water
For the Filling!
  1. 150g raspberry jam
  2. 150g ground almonds
  3. 150g butter, softened
  4. 3 eggs
  5. 150g caster sugar
  6. 1 tsp almond extract
  7. 50g flaked almonds

InstructionS

Prepare the Pastry!
  1. Mix flour and icing sugar in a bowl.
  2. Rub in the butter until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  3. Add egg yolk and cold water, mix to form a dough.
  4. Wrap in cling film and chill for 30 minutes.
Preheat the Oven!
  1. Preheat to 180°C (160°C fan)/350°F/gas mark 4.
Roll out the pastry!
  1. Roll out the chilled pastry on a floured surface.
  2. Line a 23cm tart tin with the pastry, trim the edges.
  3. Prick the base with a fork and chill for 10 minutes.
Blind bake the Pastry!
  1. Line the pastry with baking paper and fill with baking beans.
  2. Bake for 15 minutes, remove beans and paper, bake for another 5 minutes.
Prepare the filling!
  1. Spread raspberry jam over the base of the pastry.
  2. Cream butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then add almond extract.
  4. Fold in the ground almonds.
Assemble the tart!
  1. Pour the almond mixture over the jam and spread evenly.
  2. Sprinkle flaked almonds on top.
Bake the tart!
  1. Bake for 35-40 minutes until golden and set.
  2. Cool in the tin before serving.
